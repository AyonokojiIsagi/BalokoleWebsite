About FC Balokole

Founded in 2018 and proudly based in Kikuyu, Kiambu County, Kenya, FC Balokole has quickly established itself as one of the rising forces in Kenyan football. Our club represents the heart and soul of our community, bringing together players, fans, and supporters under one unified vision encapsulated in our motto: "One Spirit. One Team. One Goal."

Our Journey

2018 - Foundation

FC Balokole was established by passionate football enthusiasts in Kikuyu with a vision to develop local talent and promote football excellence in our community.

2019-2021 - Building

We invested heavily in youth development, establishing training programs and building strong foundations that would serve us for years to come.

2022-Present

Now competing at higher levels with a squad of 25 dedicated players, professional coaching staff, and unwavering community support.

Our Mission

We are committed to nurturing football talent, inspiring excellence both on and off the pitch, and serving as a beacon of hope and unity in our community. Through discipline, teamwork, and dedication, we strive to develop not just better footballers, but better people.
